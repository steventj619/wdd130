Overview

Purpose

A few years ago I went to Colorado with a couple of buddies. We tried water rafting for the first time. The thrill we felt when going down the river was exhilarating. When I came back to California, I immediately wanted to go back. There wasn't a company nearby who offered water rafting. I decided I wanted to open a water rafting company in my area so others could experience what my buddies and I had experience.

Audience

Who are the target customers? My target customers aren't those who live through a screen or read about having fun. They challenge themselves and set out to experience adventures. What do they want? My customers are the type who love the adrenaline rush How will my audience usually access my site? My site will be accessed through desktops, laptops, mobile devices
